# About Pulso Marketing

Pulso Marketing was created with the idea of helping local businesses grow through practical digital tools.

Many small businesses have great service, but their online presence does not always reflect their real value. Pulso Marketing focuses on closing that gap with websites, automation, lead capture and simple systems that help businesses look more professional and respond faster.

This project combines marketing, automation, design and business strategy.

## Vision

To help local businesses compete with a stronger digital presence and smarter customer communication.
