# Pulso Marketing

Pulso Marketing is a digital marketing and automation project focused on helping local businesses improve their online presence, capture leads and automate customer communication.

## Purpose

The goal is to provide simple, practical and modern digital solutions for local businesses that need better websites, local SEO, lead capture and automation.

## Services

- Website creation
- Local SEO
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Lead capture forms
- WhatsApp automation
- CRM setup
- Digital ads support
- AI-powered content and workflows

## Target Clients

- Dental clinics
- Local service businesses
- Real estate professionals
- Small businesses in Tijuana, Ensenada and San Diego

## Tech Stack

- Hostinger Horizon
- Supabase
- Stripe
- WhatsApp / Twilio
- Google Business Profile
- AI tools
